# Repository for the Agents Who Understand When To Plan Work

## High leel idea
We want to create agents with a few properties:
1. They understand time cost
    a. Creating a plan will take time
    b. They won't have that plan instantly
    c. They need to do something while they wait
    d. They can't just sit there (depending on domain)
2. They understand how planning works (at a high level)
    a. They can usually follow a plan, but may need to recreate one
    b. When to recreate one?
